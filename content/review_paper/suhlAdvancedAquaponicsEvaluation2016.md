#### Abstract
>Aquaponics for intensive crop production is a highly complex system in which three different biological systems (ï¬sh, plants, and nitrifying bacteria) with different requirements must be merged. Finding the right combination is a serious challenge and the dependencies avoid a high productivity until now. Therefore, a unique and innovative double recirculating aquaponic system (DRAPS) was developed as a prerequisite for a high productivity comparable to professional stand-alone ï¬sh/plant facilities. It consists of two independent recirculating units â€“ a recirculating aquaculture unit for ï¬sh production and a closed hydroponic cycle for plant production â€“ which were connected unidirectional. This allows the use of ï¬sh waste water as nutrient supply for plants in hydroponics and its optimization for plant growth by fertilizer supply without negative effects on ï¬sh rearing. Furthermore it allows a sustainable food production.
